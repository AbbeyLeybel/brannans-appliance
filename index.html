    History

    Founded in 1988, Brannan's Appliance Repair Ltd. has been serving the Greater Toronto Area for 28 years.

    Steve Brannan, the founder and owner of Brannan's Appliance, has been in the appliance repair industry since 1975, when he began fixing appliances for his family's business in his spare time, at the age of 14.

    Coming from a family of experts, Steve officially joined his grandfather's business, Harry Edwards Appliances, in 1980. There he began to repair major appliances professionally, the same work he does today.

    Television and Radio Appearances

    As part of a successful repair business, our founder regularly fielded customer inquiries, ranging from repair cost estimates to over-the-phone diagnostics of appliance issues, to what that button with the strange picture does. After five years of helping answer these questions, both in person and over the phone, Steve was inspired to bring his expertise and unique personality to a much wider audience.

    Hoping to start his own call-in show on CBC radio, the Canadian Broadcasting Centre's radio network, Steve attended a CBC open house and pitched his idea to David Schatzky, the then-host of 'Ontario Today'. The idea was quickly accepted, and in 1986 Steve Brannan started what would become a 30+ year journey of sharing his advice and knowledge with the people of the GTA through radio and television.

    He has since been featured on CBC Radio's 'Fresh Air' and 'Ontario Today', CBC Television's 'Marketplace', TVO's 'More to Life', CityTV's 'CityLine', GlobalTV's 'News at Noon', and CTV's 'Eye on Toronto' and 'The Dini Petty Show'

    Brannan's Appliance Repair Ltd.

    Two years after his first feature on Ontario Today, at the age of 27, Steve Brannan founded Brannan's Appliance Repair Ltd. With Steve as the first and only employee throughout its 28 years, Brannan's Appliance has flourished with increasing media attention and a reputation for honest, reliable service backed by decades of experience.

    His years of repair and radio experience have made Steve Brannan able to give unusually accurate repair quotes and diagnose appliance issues, including when a major appliance is better replaced than repaired, over the phone without having to set foot in your home. This unique skill, along with Steve's dedication to making sure every customer understands exactly what needs to be done to fix their particular appliance issue, and why, has helped build Brannan's Appliance the spotless reputation it has today and make us one of the most-recommended appliance repair businesses in the Greater Toronto Area.

    Certifications

    Steve Brannan has certifications as an Appliance Repair Technician from George Brown College, and as an Electronics Technician from DeVry Institute of Technology. He is constantly updating his knowledge to keep up with the newest advances in major appliance technology.
